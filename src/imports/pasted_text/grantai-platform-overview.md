Build an enterprise SaaS web application called "GrantAI."

GrantAI is an AI-powered grant intelligence, opportunity discovery, proposal management, and strategic funding platform built specifically for nonprofits, educational institutions, startups, municipalities, and small businesses.

Unlike traditional grant trackers, GrantAI understands an organization's mission, strategic priorities, products, services, previous proposals, budgets, partnerships, geography, impact metrics, and funding history to proactively identify funding opportunities and help organizations win more grants.

The design should feel like a combination of Notion, Linear, ChatGPT, Monday.com, and Bloomberg Terminal—but cleaner, modern, AI-first, and approachable.

Use a clean white interface with subtle gradients, rounded cards, interactive dashboards, beautiful charts, AI insights, and a premium SaaS feel.
